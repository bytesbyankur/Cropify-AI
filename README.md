**Cropify AI** is an advanced mobile-first diagnostic platform designed to empower rural farmers with the power of Artificial Intelligence. Developed by **Team Pixel Pirates** at **VSSUT, Burla**, this project bridges the gap between laboratory-grade agricultural science and grassroots farming.

---

## 🚨 The Challenge
Agricultural pathogens account for up to **30% of global crop losses** annually. In rural regions like Odisha, smallholder farmers face critical barriers:
* **Lack of Expertise:** Limited access to on-site agronomists leading to misdiagnosis.
* **Chemical Overuse:** Blind application of broad-spectrum pesticides, which degrades soil health.
* **Economic Vulnerability:** A single diseased harvest can lead to generational financial debt.

## 💡 Our Solution
We provide an "Agronomist in your pocket." By leveraging custom-trained **Deep Learning models**, Cropify AI allows farmers to snap a photo of a diseased leaf and receive:
1.  **Instant Identification:** High-confidence detection of specific plant pathogens.
2.  **Remedy Matrix:** Actionable treatment plans categorized into **Organic** and **Chemical** interventions.
3.  **Eco-Prescriptions:** Precise dosage guidelines to prevent environmental chemical runoff.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Diagnostic Scanner:** A sleek, glassmorphic UI for 3-second disease identification.
* **Persistent Dark Mode:** Optimized for high-visibility use in bright field conditions.
* **Responsive Landing Page:** A professional, animated showcase of the project’s mission and impact.
* **UN SDG Alignment:** Built specifically to target **Zero Hunger**, **Innovation**, and **Responsible Consumption**.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

### **Frontend**
* **React + Vite:** For a high-performance, modular user experience.
* **Tailwind CSS:** For a modern, utility-first design system.
* **Framer Motion:** For interactive "dancing" CTAs and smooth transitions.

### **Backend**
* **Django REST Framework:** Managing the secure image processing pipeline.
* **TensorFlow/Keras:** Powering the CNNs for image classification.
* **SQLite:** Storing comprehensive databases of remedies.
